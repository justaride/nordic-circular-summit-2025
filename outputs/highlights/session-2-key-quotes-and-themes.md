**Royal Greenland Case Study**:

**Transformation Strategy**:
- Shifted from shrimp meal to high-value chitosan
- Partnered through 100% Shrimp project
- Embedded circular thinking in organizational culture
- Leveraged Greenlandic cultural values

**Result**: Loss-making â†’ Profitable operation

**Quote**:
