## Conclusion: Numbers Tell The Story

Day 1 of the Nordic Circular Summit 2025 generated a rich dataset demonstrating that circular economy in the Nordic-Arctic-Baltic region is:

1. **Scaling rapidly** (45% → 90%+ utilization, €31B → €100B markets)
2. **Creating value** ($12 → $5,000 per fish, 50% cost savings on renovation)
3. **Building infrastructure** (38 outposts, 119 partners, 70+ companies, 12 countries)
4. **Driven by policy** (75+ national roadmaps, Nordic LCA harmonization, Denmark fiscal act)
5. **Rooted in place** (4 million Arctic, 56K Greenland, 50-person settlements)
6. **Supported by youth** (18 organizations, youngest-ever 30-year-old mayor)
7. **Honoring tradition** (millennia of practice, 80% biodiversity in indigenous lands)
8. **Implementing now** (8 retail products, 108 forum speakers, 34 sessions)

The numbers demonstrate what the words proclaim: the circular economy transition is underway, with concrete results, measurable impacts, and accelerating momentum.

From 4% to 25% circularity globally. From linear to circular. From waste to value. From planning to implementation.

One fish, one building, one partnership, one municipality at a time.
