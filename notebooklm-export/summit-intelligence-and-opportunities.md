# Nordic Circular Summit 2025: Strategic Intelligence & Opportunities

## Purpose

This document provides strategic analysis of opportunities emerging from the Nordic Circular Summit 2025, designed to guide Nordic Circular Hotspot (NCH) positioning and project development.

---

## Emerging Themes Requiring NCH Response

### 1. Material Security & Critical Minerals
The summit revealed growing urgency around critical raw materials governance. NCH should consider:
- Positioning on Greenland's mineral governance discussions
- Supporting circular solutions for material security
- Connecting with EU Critical Raw Materials Act implementation

### 2. Trust as Infrastructure
Multiple sessions emphasized trust as foundational infrastructure. NCH can:
- Design programs emphasizing long-term relationship building
- Facilitate pre-competitive collaboration frameworks
- Document trust-building methodologies

### 3. Traditional Knowledge Integration
Strong interest in connecting indigenous/traditional knowledge with circular economy. NCH opportunities:
- Partner with Greenlandic and Sami knowledge holders
- Document traditional circular practices
- Support knowledge transfer programs

### 4. Baltic Integration
Baltic Circular Hotspot represents major opportunity for regional expansion. NCH should:
- Deepen partnership with Agita BaltbƒÅrde and team
- Support Baltic textile transition group formation
- Explore joint funding applications

### 5. Youth Engagement
Regeneration 2030 and youth voices featured prominently. NCH could:
- Create youth advisory structure
- Support youth-led initiatives
- Include youth in governance
