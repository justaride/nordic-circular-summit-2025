# Nordic Circular Summit 2025 - Complete Context

## Event Significance

This is the **6th Nordic Circular Summit** and marks a historic milestone as the **first time the summit is held in one of the North Atlantic autonomous regions** (Greenland). Previous summits were held in Nordic capitals (2020-2023) and Helsinki during Slush week in 2024.
