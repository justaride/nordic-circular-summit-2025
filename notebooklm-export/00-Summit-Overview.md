# Nordic Circular Summit 2025 - Complete Overview

## Event Summary

**Name:** Nordic Circular Summit 2025
**Theme:** Circular Frontiers: Shaping our Future
**Tagline:** From Remote Realities to Global Blueprints: How Arctic & Nordic communities can shape the next wave of circular innovation
**Edition:** 6th Annual Summit
**Dates:** November 19-20, 2025
**Location:** Hans Egede Hotel, Nuuk, Greenland

## Historic Significance

This summit marks a **historic milestone** as the **first time the Nordic Circular Summit is held in one of the North Atlantic autonomous regions**. Previous summits were held in Nordic capitals (2020-2023) and Helsinki during Slush week in 2024.

## Format & Attendance

- **Hybrid Event:** In-person + Online

  - Online: 2,500

## Day 1 Program - November 19, 2025

### Session 1: Circular Frontiers: Shaping our Future (09:15-10:30)
**Type:** Opening Keynote
**Focus:** How entrepreneurship, circular business models, and funding can evolve the circular transition. Examines geopolitical forces creating circular momentum through localized value chains.

**Key Themes:**
- The new geopolitical situation creating circular economy momentum
- Resilience through localized value chains
- Green Deal and ESPR implementation
- Entrepreneurship as driver for circular transition
- Nordic collaboration and innovation

---

### Session 2: Circular Ocean Industries (10:45-11:45)
**Type:** Panel Discussion
**Focus:** Nordic collaboration in blue industry, exploring how ocean-based industries can embrace circular economy principles.

**Key Themes:**
- Circular principles in fisheries and ocean industries
- Nordic ocean cluster collaboration
- Marine resource optimization
- Waste reduction in fishing operations
- Sustainable blue economy business models

**Key Organizations:** Royal Greenland, Iceland Ocean Cluster, Danish Ocean Cluster, Halibut Greenland, Polar Seafood

---

### Session 3: Locally Rooted: Materialising a Circular Future (12:45-13:30)
**Type:** Panel Discussion
**Focus:** Self-sufficiency and resilient local economies. Material innovation focusing on locally sourced materials, seasonal food, and local production knowledge.

**Key Themes:**
- Nature as regenerative model for business
- Biomimicry and learning from natural systems
- Economics of nature and nature valuation
- Regenerative business models
- Local material innovation and self-sufficiency
- Seasonal food systems and Arctic resources
- Resource stewardship and finite resources

---

### Session 4: Arctic & Nordic Lifestyles (13:45-14:45)
**Type:** Panel Discussion
**Focus:** Circular traditions, culture, and knowledge embedded in Arctic and Nordic lifestyles. How traditional knowledge informs sustainable consumption.

**Key Themes:**
- Traditional knowledge and circular practices
- Textile circularity and Nordic materials (wool, qiviut)
- Behavioral change and the new normal
- Design for circular lifestyles
- Cultural values supporting circularity
- Sufficiency in consumption
- Indigenous knowledge and sustainability

---

### Session 5: Circular Cities and Regions (15:00-16:00)
**Type:** Panel Discussion
**Focus:** How digitalization and AI accelerate circular transition. Circular leadership at regional level.

**Key Themes:**
- AI and emerging technologies for circularity
- Digital Product Passports and traceability
- Data sharing and transparency
- Corporation-startup collaboration
- Circular leadership and governance
- Regional circular strategies
- Technology-enabled circular business models
- Smart city circular infrastructure

---

### Day 1 Summary: Closing Reflections (16:15-16:45)
