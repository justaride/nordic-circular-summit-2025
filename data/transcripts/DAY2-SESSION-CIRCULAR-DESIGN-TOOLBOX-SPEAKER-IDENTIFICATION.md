## SESSION OVERVIEW

### Context
The Circular Design Toolbox session was a digital partner session hosted by HI Advisory from Estonia. The toolbox was developed in partnership with the Estonian Business and Innovation Agency with EU funding.

### Key Topics Covered
1. **Context**: Linear economy crisis, planetary boundaries, donut economics
2. **Framework**: R Strategies by Ellen MacArthur Foundation
3. **Design Process**: British Design Council's Double Diamond (Understand, Define, Ideate, Develop)
4. **Toolbox Structure**: 15 tools across 4 stages + self-assessment questionnaire
5. **Key Tools Explained**:
   - Circularity Ambition (5-step ladder)
   - Circular Design Audit
   - Value Chain Mapping
   - Partner/User Personas
   - Ideation Tool (R strategies integration)
   - Circular Business Model Canvas

### Toolbox Stages & Tools
1. **Understand** (6 tools): Circularity Ambition, Circular Design Audit, Stakeholder Map, Value Chain Mapping, Research Planning, Interviews
2. **Define** (3 tools): Research Insights, Personas (User + Partner), Circular Design Brief
3. **Ideate** (3 tools): Ideation Tool, Solution Prioritization, Circular Business Model Ideation
4. **Develop** (3 tools): Value Chain Mapping (future state), Circular Business Model Canvas, Implementation Plan (100 days)

### Partner Organizations Mentioned
- HI Advisory (Estonia) - Toolbox developers
- Estonian Business and Innovation Agency - Co-developer
- European Union - Funding
- Nordic Circular Hotspot - Distribution platform
