## ğŸ”„ CONTENT CREATION WORKFLOW

### Step 1: Planning
1. Review summit program and milestones
2. Identify key moments to highlight
3. Create content calendar (2-4 weeks ahead)
4. Assign content types to dates

### Step 2: Creation
1. **Write copy**: Use existing post templates
2. **Design visuals**: Follow brand guidelines
3. **Gather assets**: Speaker photos, session descriptions
4. **Review**: Check for accuracy, tone, hashtags

### Step 3: Approval
1. Internal review by team
2. Partner approval (if featuring partners)
3. Speaker approval (if quoting speakers)
4. Final proofread

### Step 4: Scheduling
1. Upload to scheduling tool (Buffer, Hootsuite, Later)
2. Set optimal posting time
3. Add final hashtags and mentions
4. Schedule or publish

### Step 5: Engagement
1. Monitor comments within 1 hour
2. Respond to questions promptly
3. Like and reply to engagement
4. Share user-generated content

### Step 6: Analytics
1. Track engagement metrics
2. Note top-performing content
3. Adjust strategy based on data
4. Report to team weekly

## ğŸ“ BEST PRACTICES

### Writing Copy

**Do**:
- Use emojis strategically (2-4 per post)
- Include clear call-to-action ("Link in bio", "Join us online")
- Tag relevant accounts (@mentions)
- Use line breaks for readability
- Keep Instagram captions under 2,200 characters
- Keep LinkedIn posts under 1,300 characters (ideal: 500-800)

**Don't**:
- Overuse hashtags (max 3-5 relevant ones)
- Write walls of text
- Forget to proofread
- Post without a clear purpose
- Ignore engagement/comments
